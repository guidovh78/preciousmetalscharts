Why these tools are different

Most price calculators only do the obvious arithmetic on today's spot price. Ours also draw on a 50-year price archive (gold, silver and platinum back to 1976; palladium to 1998), built from open World Bank commodity data and aggregated market sources. That lets you answer the questions buyers actually have — did averaging in pay off, has gold held its value, would switching at the ratio have helped — with real numbers instead of guesses. Spot is ~10 minutes delayed; history updates daily.
