About preciousmetalscharts

Last updated 28 June 2026

preciousmetalscharts is an independent, reader-supported source of precious-metals price intelligence for private investors.

What we are

We show live, ~10-minute-delayed spot prices for gold, silver, platinum and palladium, the gold-to-silver ratio with decades of history, dealer-premium context, a private stack tracker and price alerts — in one calm, neutral place.

What we are not

We are not a dealer. We sell no metals and hold no inventory. Most precious-metals charts are run by dealers whose pages funnel you to their own products; ours is independent — the data is the same for everyone, and we have nothing to push.

Why we exist

A spot price on its own is commodity information. The value is in the context: how the metals are priced relative to each other, how much extra you pay over spot for physical, and how today compares with the last fifty years. That context is what most price pages leave out.

Editorial stance

Everything here is educational, not investment advice. We label data as delayed, we publish our methodology, and we keep commercial relationships transparent and separate from how we rank things.

Who’s behind it

preciousmetalscharts is run by a small independent editorial team. Questions, feedback or corrections are welcome at contact@preciousmetalscharts.com.
