About preciousmetalscharts

Last updated 29 June 2026

preciousmetalscharts is an independent precious-metals charts and education site for private investors — built and run by someone who has followed these markets for more than twenty years.

Why this site exists

This started as a personal project, born out of a long-standing passion for precious metals. Over more than twenty years of following gold, silver, platinum and palladium — watching the gold-to-silver ratio stretch and snap back, seeing premiums balloon in buying panics, and reading an awful lot of what passes for “analysis” online — one thing became clear: almost every price chart on the web is run by a dealer, and the page is built to move you toward whatever they happen to sell.

preciousmetalscharts is the opposite of that. The goal is simply to help people understand precious metals — what the prices mean, how the metals relate to each other, what you really pay over spot, and the role these metals have historically played in a portfolio — calmly, neutrally, and without anyone trying to sell you anything. If a curious first-time buyer and a twenty-year stacker can both look at this site and trust what they’re reading, it’s doing its job.

What you’ll find here

Live, ~10-minute-delayed spot prices for the four major precious metals; the gold-to-silver ratio with decades of history; dealer-premium context; free calculators powered by a 50-year price archive; a factual market recap; and a plain-language buying guide — in one calm, neutral place. You can also follow the markets through our free email recap.

What we are not

We are not a dealer. We sell no metals and hold no inventory. We have no product to push and no incentive to make any metal look better or worse than it is — the data here is the same for everyone.

Editorial principles

  • Independent. Not affiliated with any dealer or mint. We sell no metals.
  • Transparent. We publish our full methodology and data sources, and disclose every commercial relationship — kept strictly separate from how we rank or describe anything.
  • Accurate. Prices come from multiple sources with automatic failover and sanity checks, are clearly labelled delayed, and any figure we have not independently verified is marked as illustrative.
  • Educational, not advice. We provide data and context; we never tell you to buy or sell. We give no financial, tax or legal advice.
  • Correctable. If something is wrong or out of date, tell us and we’ll fix it.

Who’s behind it

preciousmetalscharts is an independent project run by a private precious-metals investor, not a company with something to sell. We keep the focus on the data and the education rather than on personalities. Questions, feedback or corrections are genuinely welcome — email contact@preciousmetalscharts.com.
