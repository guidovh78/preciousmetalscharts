Privacy Policy

Last updated 30 June 2026

Short version: our app collects no personal data, and the website only stores the email you choose to give us for the newsletter. We never sell your data.

The mobile app

Our iOS app collects no personal data and requires no account. It simply downloads our public, ~10-minute-delayed price data over a secure (HTTPS) connection and shows it to you. There is no tracking, no advertising identifier, and no third-party analytics SDK. Your preferences (such as currency, unit and light/dark mode) are stored only on your own device and are never sent to us.

The website

Newsletter. If you sign up for our newsletter, we store the email address and the preferences you choose (frequency and which metals). We use these only to send you the newsletter you asked for. Sign-up uses double opt-in (you confirm by email), and every email has a one-click unsubscribe. Delivery is handled by our email provider, Brevo, acting on our behalf.

Server logs. Like virtually all websites, our host keeps standard technical logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and to keep the service running. We do not use these to build profiles of you.

Affiliate links. Some links to third-party sellers are affiliate links, clearly disclosed. If you click one, that third party may set its own cookies under its own privacy policy. We are not paid by you and add no markup.

What we never do

We do not sell or rent your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising or cross-site tracking.

Your choices and rights

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link in any email, which removes your address from our list. Depending on where you live (for example under the EU GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct or delete the data we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at contact@preciousmetalscharts.com.

Children

The app and site are intended for adults and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If we update this policy we will change the date above. Material changes affecting the newsletter will be communicated by email where appropriate.

Questions about privacy? Email contact@preciousmetalscharts.com.
