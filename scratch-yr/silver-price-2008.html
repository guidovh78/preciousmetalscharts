Silver price in 2008

In 2008, silver fell −35.3%, opening the year around $16 per troy ounce and ending near $10. It averaged $15, with a high of $19.32 on 1 Mar 2008 and a low of $9.87 on 1 Nov 2008. Today silver trades around $58 — about 289% above its 2008 average.
