Precious-metals prices on your phone

Two free ways to keep live gold, silver, platinum and palladium prices in your pocket — with charts and the gold-to-silver ratio. Pick whichever suits you; both show the same data.

App Store · iPhone

The official iOS app

A native iPhone app — fast, calm and built for the job. It opens instantly from your home screen and feels right at home on iOS.

  • Live spot prices for all four metals, with the day's change
  • Tap any metal for an expanded chart — 1M, 1Y, 5Y, 50-year
  • The gold-to-silver ratio, in your currency and unit
  • Light and dark mode
  • No account, no tracking, no sign-up

How to add it to your home screen

Then it opens full-screen and works just like a real app — no download.

iPhone — in Safari
  1. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add — the icon now sits on your home screen.
Android — in Chrome
  1. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap Add to Home screen, then Add.

Not sure which? If you have an iPhone and want a true native app, go for the App Store app. If you're on Android, or you'd rather not install anything, the web app does everything the same. Both are free, independent and show identical live data.
