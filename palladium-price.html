What moves the Palladium price

Palladium is overwhelmingly an industrial metal: its main use is in autocatalysts for petrol engines. Supply is concentrated in Russia and South Africa, and the market is small — which has made palladium one of the most volatile precious metals, with sharp booms and busts.

Common questions

What is the live palladium price?

The live palladium price here is the spot price per troy ounce in US dollars, aggregated from public market data and ~10 minutes delayed, with per-gram and per-kilo shown automatically.

Why is palladium so volatile?

Palladium has a small market and its demand is dominated by petrol-engine autocatalysts, with supply concentrated in Russia and South Africa. Small shifts in supply or demand can cause large price swings.

What is palladium used for?

Mostly catalytic converters in petrol cars, plus electronics and some jewellery. Its price therefore tracks the auto industry and emissions rules closely.

What is the palladium price per gram?

Divide the troy-ounce price by 31.1035. We show the per-gram and per-kilo price automatically above.
