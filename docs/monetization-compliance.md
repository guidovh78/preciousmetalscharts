# Monetization, market & compliance

## Market context
- **Users:** retail metals investors and "stackers"; people researching a gold IRA / 401(k)
  rollover; people checking spot price, the gold:silver ratio, or coin premiums.
- **Competitors** (charts as dealer lead-magnets): APMEX, JM Bullion, SD Bullion, Kitco, Monex,
  GoldPrice.org, MetalsDaily, DailyMetalPrice, MetalCharts.org.
- **The wedge:** most competitors are dealers (charts funnel to *their* inventory) or
  cluttered/ad-heavy. Our edge is **independence + speed + clarity + AI-citability**: a clean,
  neutral tool that answer engines are comfortable quoting, with one honest, well-structured money
  page instead of a storefront.

## Monetization funnel
**free charts (trust/traffic) → email capture (alerts/newsletter) → gold-IRA referral (high
value) ; bullion affiliate (secondary) ; display ads (floor).**

| Layer | Mechanism | Rough economics | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | **Gold-IRA lead-gen / affiliate** | ~$25–$500 per qualified lead; or rev-share on a ~$40k avg investment. One conversion = hundreds–thousands. | The `gold-ira` page is the money page. |
| Secondary | **Bullion affiliate** (coins/bars) | ~3–7% commission | Contextual links from premiums/coin content. |
| Floor | **Display ads** | low CPM | Only if it doesn't hurt speed/trust. Keep minimal. |

## Compliance & content guardrails — non-negotiable
1. **No financial, tax, or legal advice.** Educate and refer only. State this in the footer.
2. **Never publish invented figures about real companies.** Every gold-IRA score, fee, minimum,
   and claim must be **independently verified** before publishing. Current comparison numbers are
   **illustrative placeholders** — replace them. If unverified, don't ship the figure.
3. **Affiliate disclosure** (FTC-style) near any ranking + in the footer; `rel="sponsored
   nofollow"` on affiliate links; scoring weights fixed **before** commissions and disclosed.
   Commissions must never influence rank.
4. **Label data "delayed"** ("~10 min delayed"); don't imply real-time/live.
5. **Honor source attribution** where the free tier requires it (e.g. goldpricez visible credit).
   The pipeline carries `attributions`; the client renders them — don't strip.
6. **Risk language:** metals carry risk incl. loss of principal and pay no yield; past performance
   ≠ future results.
7. **Home-storage gold IRA = warning sign** (IRS requires an approved depository) — state plainly
   wherever relevant; it's correct and a trust signal.
