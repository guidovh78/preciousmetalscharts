How to add it to your website

The widget is a standard iframe, so it works anywhere you can paste HTML — a website, a blog, or a CMS like WordPress, Ghost, Webflow or Squarespace (use an “HTML” or “embed” block). Choose your options above, copy the snippet, and paste it where you want the prices to appear. It loads its own live data and refreshes automatically about every minute — nothing to maintain.

02

Common questions

Is the widget free?

Yes — free to embed on any website, with no account and no API key. It shows live gold, silver, platinum and palladium spot prices, ~10 minutes delayed, and updates automatically.

Where do the prices come from?

From our own price feed — the same spot data used across this site, aggregated from public market sources and refreshed about every 10 minutes. It is a reference price, not a real-time trading feed.

Will it slow down my site?

No. It loads in an isolated iframe with its own lightweight code, so it can't affect your page's styling or scripts, and it loads lazily.

Can I keep the attribution link?

Please do — the small “by preciousmetalscharts” link is how the widget stays free. It's a normal, branded link to our homepage.
